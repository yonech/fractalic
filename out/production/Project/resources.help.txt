To create a fractal choose Select > Fractal

Input names into the text field to create new fractals.


When you select Edit, you can define how your fractal looks in each recursive step.

The grey and orange dot represent the beginning and end of the edited type.

The arrows represent what the fractal changes to in each recursive step.

To add arrows, select one of the fragment types and drag your mouse across the screen.

To delete an arrow right click on it.



To draw a fractal just drag your mouse from its beginning to its end on the canvas.
